Tailored Guidance

What to Include in Your Message

To help Leah give you the most relevant and timely response, here is what to include depending on the nature of your enquiry.

For Coaching

  • What area you want to work on (life, career, business, leadership)
  • Where you are right now vs. where you want to be
  • Whether you prefer 1:1 or group coaching
  • Your availability and preferred session frequency
  • Any previous coaching experience (optional)

For Administration / Hire

  • Your organisation type and size
  • The specific role or function you need support with
  • Whether the engagement is full-time, part-time, or project-based
  • Expected start date and duration
  • Any relevant industry context

For Fashion & Styling

  • The occasion or context (everyday, corporate, event, media)
  • Your current style challenges or goals
  • Whether you need a wardrobe audit, personal shopping, or image consulting
  • Your general body type and comfort level with fashion
  • Any specific cultural or dress code considerations

For Speaking & Events

  • Event name, date, and location
  • Your target audience and expected attendance
  • Preferred topic or theme (gender, leadership, coaching, fashion)
  • Format — keynote, panel, workshop, or fireside chat
  • Speaking fee budget range

For Writing & Content

  • The type of content needed (article, blog, copy, newsletter)
  • The platform or publication it is intended for
  • Your target audience and desired tone
  • Word count and deadline
  • Any reference content or style guides

For Nonprofit & Governance

  • Your organisation's mission and current stage
  • The specific governance or programme challenge you are facing
  • Whether you need board advisory, M&E support, or fundraising strategy
  • The size of your team and funding context
  • Any existing frameworks or reports to review
