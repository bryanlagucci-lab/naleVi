Who She Is

A Story of Purpose

Nabukeera Leah Victoria — widely known as NALEVI — is a dynamic, multi-disciplinary Ugandan professional whose career is defined by an unwavering commitment to empowerment, creativity, and purposeful leadership. Born and raised in the vibrant city of Kampala, she grew up surrounded by stories of resilience, community spirit, and the rich cultural tapestry of East Africa — values that continue to anchor everything she does.

Over a decade ago, Leah stepped into the professional arena armed with a passion for people and an instinct for organisation. What began as a career in office administration quickly evolved into a multifaceted professional identity. She discovered that her greatest strength lay not in performing a single role, but in weaving together disciplines that most people keep separate — business, coaching, fashion, writing, advocacy, and governance.

As a Business Coach and Life Coach, she has guided entrepreneurs, executives, and young professionals through pivotal transitions, helping them crystallise their goals and move with decisive clarity. Her coaching philosophy is grounded in empathy — she meets people exactly where they are, then helps them discover the version of themselves they are reaching for.

Her love for fashion and style is not superficial; it is deeply tied to identity, culture, and confidence. As a stylist and fashion advocate, Leah champions the idea that how we present ourselves communicates our story before we speak a word. She uses fashion as a tool for empowerment, particularly for women navigating professional spaces.

As a writer and blogger, she documents her observations on tourism, lifestyle, and social issues with a voice that is both intelligent and accessible. Her advocacy work — particularly for gender inclusion and humanitarian causes — rounds out a career that refuses to be confined to a single title or industry.

NALEVI is, at her core, a builder of people. Whether in a boardroom, a coaching session, a fashion event, or a community forum, she brings the same energy: intentional, warm, and transformative.
